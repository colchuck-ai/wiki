# CR003 - Concept verb surface: create, revise, merge

Reshaped the concept write operations into an intent-named verb surface: C003's `author(keep-new|merge)` becomes `create` + `revise` with the material source as a parameter, `merge` becomes a `revise`+`delete` composition, and index and recency maintenance become internal effects of each write verb. No named read verbs are added — reading the plain-text corpus stays a direct consumption convenience — and the one read change is redefining `history(concept_id)` so it stays complete across a relocation.

## Change

Before: the concept operations were organized by owning component and welded to where their material came from. C003 exposed a single `author(task_id)` with a `direction.mode ∈ { keep-new, merge }`, so the only way to change a concept's content was to fold in a *new admitted task* — there was no way to correct or refine an existing concept from the steward's direction (a path C006 and C010 already referenced but that had no interface), and no way to combine two concepts already in the corpus. `reindex` (C005) and `record` (C007) were terminal calls every writer had to remember to make, and a bypass drifted the index or recency silently. `history(concept_id)` (C007) was scoped to the concept's current directory and silently truncated after a relocation. The single-concept read and the index read were unnamed ("Query is a consumption convenience").

After: the write surface is five intent-named verbs — `create`, `revise`, `move`, `delete`, `deprecate` — with `merge` as a named composition of `revise` + `delete`. Material source is a parameter of `create`/`revise`: an admitted task, another concept, or (for `revise` of an existing concept) the steward's direction. `create` mints a new concept; `revise` folds a source into an existing one in place, preserving id, history, and inbound links; `merge` combines two existing concepts. Each write verb performs its own recency/history recording (C007) and index regeneration (C005) as internal effects, so no caller orchestrates them. No dedicated read verbs are added: the Query consumption operation reads the plain-text concepts and the materialized `index.md` directly, and the reads that compute (`history`/`history_all`, `inbound_links`, `dangling_links`, `graph`) stay on their components. The one read change is redefining `history(concept_id)` (C007) as a bundle-wide filtered scan, complete regardless of relocation. C003, C005, C007, and the architecture README are updated accordingly. Only source-less `create` (a new concept from the steward's own knowledge with no task) is deferred to a future PDR; `create` stays admitted-task-gated.

## Rationale

A CRUD-coverage review of the concept lifecycle surfaced the shape of the problem: because the verbs fused *source of material* with *lifecycle transition*, two intent-cells were left with no interface at all — revising an existing concept from steward direction (the C006/C010 remediation path) and merging two existing concepts (the in-corpus case of the O003-R003 duplication choice, which already names "merge"). Decoupling the two axes fills both cells with a smaller verb set. Making materialization a verb effect removes the correctness-by-convention footgun that let the index and recency drift. Naming the reads and completing `history` close the smaller gaps the same review found. See [ADR011](../engineering/drs/ADR011-concept-verb-surface.md) for the full option analysis, including why keeping the component-ownership decomposition and treating `merge` as a fourth primitive were both rejected.

## Affects

- **C003 - Integration Authoring** — the primary modified element: `author(keep-new|merge)` becomes `create` and `revise`, and it hosts the `merge` composition; `promote_source`/`propose_links`/`record`/`reindex` become internal effects. Its Data model, Interfaces, Behavior, Edge cases, Relationships, Success criteria, and Notes are updated.
- **C005 - Index & Navigation** — `reindex` becomes an internal verb effect (no `list` read verb is added; the materialized `index.md` is read directly); its Interfaces, Behavior, Edge cases, and Relationships are updated.
- **C007 - Currency Tracking** — `record` becomes an internal verb effect and `history(concept_id)` is redefined bundle-wide and complete; its Interfaces, Behavior, Edge cases, Relationships, and Success criteria are updated.
- **Wiki Curation Architecture** — the Agent-Skill operation vocabulary is re-expressed in the verb surface, the "never dead-end" principle is refined, a "write verbs carry their own effects" principle is added, and a new **Operations** section maps Ingest / Survey / Lint / Query to the component operations that back them (making the detect-then-remediate loop explicit).
- **O004-R003 - Assisted upkeep** — now has a concrete interface for correcting an existing concept by intent (`revise` from steward direction) rather than by hand-editing.
- **O002-R002 - Change history** — served more completely: per-concept history no longer truncates when a concept is relocated.
- **ADR011** — records the decision and the alternatives considered; supersedes ADR005's `author`-mode framing in part.
